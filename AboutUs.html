DoublePunch was launched in April 2020 with the goal of reporting on the gaming industry from the view of ordinary gamers. DoublePunch wants to be able to report on the gaming industry objectively without bias, all while entertaining our readers at the same time.

Founders Sam Fisher and Gordon Freeman are college buddies who love to play games together, but felt that current reporting on the gaming industry was missing something. It was missing the objectiveness that regular news reporting cherishes so deeply. So Sam and Gordon set out to cover news in the gaming industry that was to the same standard as mainstream news such as the Associated Press and the BBC. DoublePunch strives for quality reporting that informs ordinary gamers on information that deserves to be seen by them.

Two years later, this spontaneous decision during Spring Break in the middle of a pandemic has proven a success story for two college roomates. DoublePunch became an instant hit and is now a recogizable name in the gaming industry and among ordinary gamers looking for news on the next game or looking for tutorials in long cherished titles.
